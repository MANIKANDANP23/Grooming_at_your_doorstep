About Us

Our Services

At HOMIES, we offer a wide range of home services to cater to your personal care needs:

  • Haircuts: Whether you're looking for a trendy new style, a classic cut, or a simple trim, our experienced hairstylists are ready to help you achieve the look you desire.

  • Henna Artistry: Our skilled henna artists specialize in creating beautiful and intricate designs for all occasions, from weddings and festivals to personal adornment.

  • Makeup Artistry: From natural, everyday looks to glamorous makeovers for special events, our professional makeup artists use high-quality products and techniques to enhance your natural beauty.

Our Team

As a young and dynamic company, our team consists of certified and passionate professionals dedicated to providing you with the best possible service. Each member of the HOMIES team is carefully selected and trained to ensure they meet our high standards of quality, hygiene, and customer care. We take pride in our work and strive to make every appointment a relaxing and enjoyable experience for our clients.

  • Personalized Care: We tailor our services to meet your individual needs and preferences, ensuring a personalized and satisfying experience.

Join Us on Our Journey
